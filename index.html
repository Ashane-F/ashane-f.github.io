About: I consider myself a student of life in three suits, i.e., Engineer, Researcher and Lecturer. Over the years, I have learned a great deal, yet I recognise there is always more to learn. I enjoy explaining and teaching concepts using mathematics, and I constantly seek a deeper understanding of challenging mathematics to improve my own knowledge.

Research: I have keen interests in exploring opportunities in minerals processing (understand and optimise processes), waste management (promote circular economy), image processing (theory and applications) and Chemical Engineering (reactions, fluids and thermodynamics). I pursue Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML) very close to my heart (as an interested and keen learner) and eagerly wait to propose meaningful data-driven solutions to questions as my endeavour. I am always open to interesting ideas and I invite you to connect with me for fruitful discussions :).
